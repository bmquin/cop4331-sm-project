What is COPY?

COPY stands for "Contact Organizer for POOSD with Yadavally." COPY is the website built by Team 1 for the Small Project assignment required by Processes of Object Oriented Software Design (aka POOSD or COP 4331) at the University of Central Florida (UCF). This course is being taught by Dr. Yadavally during the Spring 2026 semester. This website implements the LAMP tech stack (Linux, Apache, MySQL, and PHP) and is hosted through a domain purchased from (fill in later).

COPY was designed to be an easy way to manage your own personal contact list. COPY supports the following features:
  • Adding new contacts
  • Deleting existing contacts
  • Editing existing contacts
  • Searching through contacts (including partial matching)

COPY was developed by a team of 6 undergraduate Computer Science students at The University of Central Florida in Orlando, FL. They are:
