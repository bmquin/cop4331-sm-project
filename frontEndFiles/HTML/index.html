What is COPY?

COPY stands for The Contact Organizer for POOSD with Yadavally. COPY is an implementation of a contact manager for POOSD (Processes with Object Oriented Software Development) to satisfy the "Small Project" for the course with Dr. Yadavally at The University of Central Florida (UCF).

Designed in the early months of 2026, COPY was built by a team of 6 undergraduate Computer Science students. COPY is an easy way to manage your own personal contact list. COPY supports:

  • Adding new contacts
  • Deleting existing contacts
  • Editing existing contacts
  • Searching through contacts (including with partial matching!)
Basically, it's a way to store the information of those you care about, without having to remember any of their information :)
